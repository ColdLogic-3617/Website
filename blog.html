Our Website is Live!

October 16th, 2014

After four years, we have finally secured hosting for our website. We'd like to thank the Northern Michigan University department of Mathematics & Computer Science for allowing us to use their server. While we put the rest of the site together, there are still some things that you can do. If you go to the "Schedule" section, you can see all our events, kept a few weeks ahead. Or if you scroll to the bottom, you can donate to our team. We're currently experimenting with Bitcoin, so we would appreciate input.

Thank you for your time.
