Who are we?

Formed in 2010, FRC Team #3617 or Team Cold Logic as our members prefer to be called is a consortium of local students and mentors who share in the experieince of building and competing with robots within the confines of FIRST which serves as our parent organisation. Following the January kickoff event MSHS students, teaching staff, parent volunteers, industry mentors, and University staff have six weeks to brainstorm, design, build, and test a single funcitoning robot to compete against other Michigan high school teams in this year's competition.

Team Cold Logic Mission Statement!

It is our shared vision that participation in FIRST serves as a mechanism for helping students and mentors reach higher levels of peronal and academic achievement characteristically attained in competitive events outside of the classroom. Impacts of FRC on students include:

  • Increased understanding of science & technology in everyday life
  • Learning new and practical work-related skills through teamwork
  • Increased value and understanding of teamwork
  • Increase self-confidence
  • Ability to build upon team success while enabling future career interest in science & technology

Compared to similar groupings in terms of backgrounds and achievements in high school, FIRST alumni are significantly more likely to attend college and twice as likely to major in science and engineering fields.
